what even is fast fashion?

think about all the amazing and wonderful clothing you buy in a year. a month. a week. all these amazing trends and fashion styles that come and go. pretty nice right? well it is nice until you think about the amount of waste it creates yearly. americans alone - 16 million tons of textile waste. pretty bad ain't it?

why should i care?

everyone lives on this Earth. everyone has their own role in taking care of it. fast fashion causes mass polution in landfills due to people rapidly disposing of the clothing when the trends end.
